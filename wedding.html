Wedding & Ceremonies

A wedding ceremony is the party of the couple at a certain time. It is a sort of feast on a marriage. A wedding ceremony is generally held in a community center. Those who are not financially solvent arrange this ceremony at home. It is held generally in the afternoon or in the evening. The community center is decorated with various colored lights and colored canopy. Guests attend the ceremony in their beautiful dress and gift for the new bridegroom and the bride is the center of attraction of a wedding.
